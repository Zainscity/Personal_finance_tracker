
## Goal
Provide personalized, actionable financial recommendations to users.

## Learning Focus
- Rule-based systems
- Data-driven insights
- User behavior analysis
- Proactive suggestions

## Fintech Concepts
- **Recommendation Engine**: System suggesting actions/products
- **Financial Nudges**: Gentle prompts to encourage good behavior
- **Personalized Insights**: Tailored advice based on individual data
- **Predictive Analytics**: Forecasting future financial states

## Features to Build

### 1. Spending Recommendations

Analyze spending patterns and suggest:
- Categories where spending can be reduced.
- Alternatives for frequent expenses (e.g., "Consider packing lunch instead of eating out daily").
- Alerts for unusual spending spikes.

### 2. Savings Recommendations

Based on income, expenses, and goals:
- Suggest a realistic monthly savings target.
- Recommend transferring a specific amount to savings.
- Highlight potential savings from cutting down on certain expenses.

### 3. Budget Adherence Tips

If a budget is set (future feature):
- Provide tips to stay within budget for specific categories.
- Warn if approaching budget limits.

### 4. Financial Health Tips

Based on overall financial health score (future feature):
- General advice for improving financial wellness.
- Suggestions for increasing income or reducing debt.

## Success Criteria

✅ Generates personalized spending reduction suggestions.
✅ Recommends realistic savings targets.
✅ Provides actionable tips for budget adherence.
✅ Offers general advice for financial health improvement.
